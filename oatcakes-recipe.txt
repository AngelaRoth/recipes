OATCAKES
Preheat oven to 300F.  Grease a baking sheet.

In a mixing bowl, combine:
  1 cup medium oatmeal
  1/2 tsp salt

In a small saucepan, bring to a boil:
  5 tbsp water
  1 tbsp butter

Add boiled water mix to oatmeal mix and knead into a ball.
Dust with oatmeal and roll out thinly on baking sheet (these are like crackers)
Score raw dough into cracker-sized pieces
Bake for 50-60 minutes until crisp.
Leave to cool on baking sheet, then remove.
